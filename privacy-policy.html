Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 28, 2026

This privacy policy applies to all mobile applications ("Apps") developed and published by Vandit Tyagi ("we", "us", or "our") on the Google Play Store.

Information We Collect

Our apps are designed with your privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. All calculations, data, and preferences are stored locally on your device only.

Advertising

Our apps use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use certain data to provide personalized ads, including:

  • Device identifiers (Advertising ID)
  • IP address
  • Device information (model, OS version)
  • General location (country/region level)

You can opt out of personalized advertising in your device settings under Google > Ads > Opt out of Ads Personalization.

For more information, see Google's Privacy Policy.

Data Storage

All app data (saved calculations, preferences, history) is stored locally on your device using SharedPreferences. This data is never transmitted to any server. If you uninstall the app, all local data is deleted.

Camera Permission (QR Scanner only)

Our QR Scanner app requires camera access solely for the purpose of scanning QR codes and barcodes. Camera data is processed locally on your device and is never stored, recorded, or transmitted.

Internet Permission

Internet access is used solely for loading advertisements and, in some apps, fetching publicly available data (e.g., wallpaper images, price data). No personal data is sent over the internet.

Children's Privacy

Our apps are not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Third-Party Services

Our apps may use the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob - for displaying advertisements
  • Pexels API - for wallpaper images (Wallpaper app only)

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
